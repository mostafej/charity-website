Our Donate Plan

After a devasting flood in early June that affected arounds 4 million residents. So, we plan some ideas for this area families.

Food for a family

Familiar food and safe water are more important in flood disaster.They lift morale and give a feeling of security in times of stress.Try to include foods that they will enjoy and that are also high in calories and nutrition.

Education for a child

Education in emergencies provides children, and their communities, with life-saving information, including disaster preparedness, awareness of their rights and responsibilities, landmine and earthquake awareness, hygiene, and other survival skills specific to their context.

Shelter for a family

Many people lost their homes, and it was crucial to construct transitional shelters for some of the most vulnerable people affected by the flood.The woes that come with the floods are not as painful as is the pain of living the life of a destitute.
